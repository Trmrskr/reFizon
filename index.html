About

Our mission is to make accessible past questions of notable examination bodies easily accessible for intending candidates and in so doing drive up the success rate of such candidates. To achieve this we are creating a bank of past questions which can easily be retrieved in times of need.

This project is inspired by the inability of candidates to access questions of remote but reputable examination bodies.
